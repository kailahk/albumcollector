About Album Collector

Album Collector is a web application created by Kailah Korsh with the intention of giving users a private digital space to create lists of albums. Many music lovers associate different albums together for a variety of reasons, and this gives them a space to dynamically create and edit lists of albums without crowding the streaming platform where they keep their saved music and playlists.
